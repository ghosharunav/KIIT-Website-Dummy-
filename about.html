About Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT)

Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT), located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, is a premier private university known for academic excellence, innovation, and global engagement. Established with the vision of transforming society through education, KIIT offers programs in engineering, medicine, management, law, humanities, sciences, and emerging technologies.

The university emphasizes outcome-based education, research-driven learning, and holistic development. With modern infrastructure, advanced laboratories, experienced faculty, and strong industry linkages, KIIT prepares students for global challenges. Its commitment to social responsibility is reflected through initiatives like the KISS Foundation, empowering underprivileged communities through education and healthcare.
