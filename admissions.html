Admissions at KIIT

KIITEE Entrance Examination

  • Primary entrance exam for UG and PG admissions.
  • Online examination evaluating program-specific aptitude.
  • Merit-based admissions followed by counseling.
  • Ensures transparent and fair selection process.

Scholarship Exams

  • Merit-Based Scholarships
  • Sports Scholarships
  • Financial Assistance Schemes
  • Special Category Scholarships

Payment & Fee Details

Application fees and admission payments can be made securely using debit cards, credit cards, and net banking. KIIT maintains a transparent fee structure which is communicated during counseling and varies by program and category.
